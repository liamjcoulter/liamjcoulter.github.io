About Me

I am a researcher in the Spectrum Dominance group at RTX BBN Technologies. I have been concurrently pursuing my Ph.D. in electrical and computer engineering at at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities since 2020, where I am advised by Dr. Jarvis D. Haupt. My research interests include statistical signal processing, image processing and computational imaging, estimation and filtering theory, and information theory.
